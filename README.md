<h1>Media Coverage Explorer</h1>

![Media Coverage Explorer](https://github.com/NYU-CS6313-SPRING2016/Group-10-INET-DOJ-Civil-Rights/blob/gh-pages/InfoVisScreenshot.png)

Interactive visualization that enables the user to view 2015 average news article tone, and number of news articles identified for each person who died while in police custody in 2015, as reported by The Counted project. Selecting each data point brings back the individualâ€™s demographic information and list of source articles. Individuals may be grouped by demographic information and ordered and characterized by their race, avg tone of articles and number of articles identified. The grouping and characterizations allow the user to locate for further review apparent patterns in media coverage, defined as tone and volume of articles, among individuals in the dataset.
